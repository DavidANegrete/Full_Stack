Helping is what we do

Lost and Found Pets in El Paso helps pets and pet owners in our community reunite when they become separated. We accomplish our mission by using social media to create awareness in the El Paso metro area.

We are Social

We use social media marketing to create awareness in our community when a pet is lost or found.

How you can help

The easiest way to help is to simply like our Facebook page, and like, share or react to one of our posts. When you take those actions, you help spread the word.

You help us with funding to our gofundme campaign.

Finding your Pet

Get the Word Out

Letting your friends and family know through social media or any other means will maximize the amount of eyes that can help you with your search.

Searching

When you have a family member roaming the streets you want to be as effective as possible to reduce the time they are in danger. A good start to your search is with other local pages groups and agencies.

Posts that work

When selecting images for your posts or flyer's, choose full body photo’s that capture distinctive features of your loved one. Include any facts pertaining to your post such as, the cross streets (or area your pet was lost), breed, color and name, and any distinguishing features. Include all facts that will make it easy to identify your pet.

Running an Ad

What is an Ad?

Ads give people the power to boost posts so they show up as 'Sponsored' on our follower’s and their friend’s news feeds. We will target a five mile radius around the location provided. If we are running an Ad for you, we will also pin your post to the top of our news feed to maximize exposure. We may also choose to use the picture provided as our profile picture to create even greater exposure. An Ad is a tool you can use to reach more people.

Maximize a Post's Reach

When a page shares a post, it gets shared along with any other posts created by Pages and people. Because people usually have lots of content displayed, posts sometimes are not seen. When we run an Ad on a post it is displayed at the top of news feeds. With an, Ad people that live in our target area will see that post as 'Sponsored'.

Area Specific

When we create a post, it will be shared with all our page followers and it may be seen by some, but missed by others if they did not scroll down their news feed far enough to see it. When we run an Ad, the vast majority of people will still see it based on where the post lands on their news feed, but a select few that matter the most will see it as 'Sponsored' because of where they live or are at.

Run it!

Enter the name of the person that created the post. You will receive a confirmation email and a reach report at the end of the Ad campaign.

FAQ's

Frequently asked questions.

How do I use your page?

Simply by following us on Facebook. Anyone can post on our page to get help with a pet that is found or is lost. Once you go to our home page and post to it, you will be able to see your post in the Visitor Posts section of our page. It will be there until we share your post with our page or remove it.

Why was my post deleted?

That post did not meet our rules and it was removed. Keep in mind that any post that does not follow page our user post rules will get removed.

Do I need run an Ad to use your page?

No, an Ad is an option available for our followers and in now way a must. What an Ad does is that it gives us greater reach. We can insure that a post is on top of our followers and their friends news feeds. This option has shown a 45% success rate!

How do I get my pet back?

If your pet has been found, congrats! You have two ways of getting in contact with the person that found your pet, you can use Facebook Messages to send that person an IM or reply on the original post and tag the persons name in your text so they get notified. Sometimes phone numbers are included too.

Can I re-home a pet on your page?

Our page is not intended to be used to re-home pets, however if you found a pet and you are attempting to find its owners or someone that can help foster the pet while its family is found is fine.

Post Rules

The following rules apply to all Posts on our page. We reserve the right to remove any image that does not follow our page rules.

  • 1. Images or videos must be original. (No Screen Shots or Images of other Posts)
  • 2. DRY images. (Posts will not be shared if they contain the same image from a previous post)
  • 3. All posts should meant be to find a pet or notify the page of a found one in the El Paso metro area.
  • 4. The location where the pet was lost or found must be included.
