About our Nail Art Studio

D'Licious Nails is about creating works of art every time you get your nails done. We offer everything you need to make your hands and feet look their best. All you have to do is to choose your artist and leave the rest up to us.

There are many in and out salons that don't deliver what their customers want. We take our time to figure out what it is that a client wants. We are a one of a kind nail art studio with the goal of exceeding our clients expectations every time. Below are the types of artist that you can find at our studio.

Advanced Artists have gone the extra mile to perfect their skills. Appointments made easy

We make it easy for you to book and change an appointment with us. We know things can happen so we give you up to 24 hours before your appointment starts to change it.

When you book with us you will receive an email notification confirming your appointment. We will also send you reminders because the last thing we want to happen is for you to miss it and get charged for not showing up. When you get reminders from us, that is a good time for you to think about what you are looking to get. We encourage you to save any images of nail art that you like and themes you want on your new set. Reach out to your artist with any questions or concerns, we want you to have a great experience with us.

FAQ's

We want you to be happy with your nails and your overall experience with us. Here are some frequently asked questions from our customers, if you feel like your question is still unanswered reach out to us by email we will be happy to answer it for you.

How much time do I book for?
A good rule of thumb when booking is to compare some our previous work with the one you are aiming to get done. Look on our social media accounts and you will find the amount of time and the name of the artist that did the work on each post #hashTagged on the comments. If it is still unclear to you, feel free email the artist or message us. Come appointment time we want for you to get the work you expect to get done and nothing less.
How much more time should I add to get the work I have now removed?
If you have work on your nails come appointment time, it will add no less than 30 minutes to your appointment. Some ladies will try to remove their existing nails themselves because they want to save time to get more work done but that is not always the best move. If it is not done with care a do it yourself removal can cause damage on the nail bed. If the bed is damaged we will not be able to work on your nails. A better option is to get them removed professionally at a nail salon and come in with a blank slate.
How can I make an appointment?
Booking an appointment is easy and all that you need is a credit card to secure your appointment and an email address where we can send notifications to. You will review a confirmation email from us that will give you the option to change, cancel or reschedule your appointment as long as it is 24 hours prior to your appointment time. If you miss it you will get charged a 30 dollar cancellation fee on the card you provide us.
Do you accept walk-ins?
We sure do and the booking is done the same way you would book for an appointment that is a few weeks ahead. The only difference is that an appointment booked on the same day does not give the option to cancel or change it, since the appointment would be starting in less than 24 hours.
How much is a new set?
We charge by the hour either 60 or 40 depending on the artist that will do the work. A set can take longer if you want intricate art work. It is best to determine what you are looking for and compare that to our previous work to get a good estimate of how long it will take.
I'm a nail bitter and would like pretty nails, can you do them for me?
We have been able to help ladies that want to beautify their nails even after having bit them off. The amount of time needed may very depending on the damage. For this type of situation it is best contact us, we will need a picture of your nails as they are now so we can determine how long it will take and if we can do the work for you.
When are you available?
Our availability is available for all to see and varies by artist, it can be found by going through the process of booking an appointment. When picking the time, you will be shown the slots available for that selected time by day.
