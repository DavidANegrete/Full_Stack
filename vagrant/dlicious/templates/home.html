What we are about

Nail art the way you want it!

We are a not your average "nail salon" we are a nail art studio. Our artists specialize in creating unique works of art for each guest.

Nail Art Pricing

Like many other artist do, we set the price for a piece of art according to the time, effort and skill needed to complete it. Skill varies from artist to artist and so do our hourly rates. There are five different artist types representing our studio, from the very advanced Master Artist Educator to Junior Nail Artists that are starting their nail art career. One thing remains consistent with all artist at our studio and that is their desire to get better at their craft.

Types of Artists

  • Junior Nail Artist
  • Nail Artist
  • Advanced Nail Artist
  • Master Nail Artist
  • Master Artist Educator
